What is Vault‑X (VX)?

Vault‑X (VX) is a digital currency powering the Vault X ecosystem. VX is designed to enable low‑latency transactions and frictionless integration with stocks and options workflows inside the app. Users can invest in Vault‑X by buying stocks and options through the Vault‑X wallet.
